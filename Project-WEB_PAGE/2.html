We completed the accreditation process and became a University

On our 25th birthday!

Algebra, having received a permit and a positive accreditation recommendation of the Agency for Science and Higher Education and a final positive opinion, and formally became the first private university in Croatia that puts the digital professional paradigm in the focus of student development of the future. The Algebra University mentoring program was carried out by the University of Zagreb through its component Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing (FER). Algebra's mission is to develop a dynamic and innovative environment for learning that empowers students and attendees to be leaders in the development and application of digital technologies in different areas.
