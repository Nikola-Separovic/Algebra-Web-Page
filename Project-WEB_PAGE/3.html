IT education for a safer tomorrow

With us into the future!

The "IT education for a safer tomorrow" project, completed after three years, provided an opportunity for individuals throughout Croatia to acquire additional digital skills and knowledge, as well as relevant certificates in the field of computing and informatics, which potentially opened up employment opportunities for them in more attractive and better-paid jobs. places. The project "IT education for a safer tomorrow", lasting from January 25, 2018 to January 25, 2021, was financed by the European Union from the European Social Fund (ESF) with the aim of educating individuals in the field of digital skills, with a special emphasis on socially vulnerable groups such as unemployed persons or those with insufficient levels of education. The total value of the project is HRK 1,332,994.50 and it was implemented in cooperation with partners, HZZ Regional Offices Sisak, Vukovar and Rijeka.
