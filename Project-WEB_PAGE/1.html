We started the new academic year with a record number of 2,000 enrolled students

New Record!

We are starting the new academic year with a record number of 2,000 enrolled students, making us one of the five largest Croatian institutions that conduct professional studies. The increase in the number of freshmen by as much as 15% compared to the last academic year is a clear signal that we are an institution that probably enjoys the greatest increase in the confidence of young people who aspire to digital careers. Algebra's Welcome day marked the beginning of the academic year 2023/2024, and was held for the second year in a row at Spark Event Space, on the Zagreb campus of Algebra - the most modern and largest private educational infrastructure in the Republic of Croatia, which covers more than 12,000 square meters. At the festive event, 650 domestic and foreign freshmen and about fifty international exchange students gathered, who filled the campus space and confirmed that the interest in innovative educational programs among new generations is even more intense.
